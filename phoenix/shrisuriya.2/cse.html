About Department





Department of Computer Science and Engineering, established in 2008 aims to prepare students as industry ready professionals and helps them to learn the trending technology at its peak of growth. We are committed in providing broad-based, innovative and high quality education with the exposure to interdisciplinary streams. The department also offers futuristic technology training for faculty to enrich themselves and equip our students at ease. The department holds a deeper industry bonding which helps our students to excel not only in academics but also facilitates a hands-on experience on the technology required for the day.
