About Department





Artificial intelligence aims at creating intelligent machines that can learn from data and exhibit human intelligence. Today, all the major applications produce massive amount of data called the “Big data”. There is high demand for skilled software professionals , the “Data Scientists” who can interpret and predict from big data. This department offers a diversified and cross disciplinary curriculum which combines Artificial intelligence and Data science technologies with a strong foundation in the core concepts of computer Science with the objective to impart students the required knowledge in intelligent data analysis and make them futuristic Engineers. The department aims in providing high quality education with comprehensive curriculum that contributes for the immediate deployment of Students. The department has well equipped laboratories and a dedicated band of qualified faculty who have constantly upgraded their skills in emerging technologies and are also certified by Industry. The students of this department will experience a holistic education that would enable them to build intelligent machines combining the latest developments in Machine Learning and Data analytics.
